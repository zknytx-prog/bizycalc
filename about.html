About BizyCalc

BizyCalc.com provides free, easy-to-use calculators and tools built for small business owners, freelancers, and independent contractors. Our goal is simple: give you fast, accurate answers to everyday business math — pricing, invoicing, break-even points, and more — without spreadsheets or signups. We add new tools regularly based on what business owners actually need.

Why we built this

Most business calculators online are either buried in ads, hidden behind a signup wall, or built for accountants instead of the person actually running the business. BizyCalc is built to be fast: open a tool, enter your numbers, get an answer — no account, no email capture, no spreadsheet template to download and mess up.

Who this is for

Freelancers pricing their time, small business owners checking margins before setting a price, and anyone who needs a quick, accurate business number without opening Excel. Every tool on this site is built around a real, common decision — hiring, pricing, borrowing, or invoicing.

FAQ

Is BizyCalc really free? Yes — every calculator is free to use with no signup, no email required, and no usage limits.

Do you store the numbers I enter? No. Calculations run entirely in your browser — nothing you type into a calculator is sent to or stored on our servers.

Can I suggest a new calculator? Yes — we add tools based on what business owners actually ask for. Reach out through the site with your suggestion.
